Overview

A compact bike-mounted speedometer designed to track current speed, maximum speed, and total distance traveled.

Design Motivation

The goal was to create a minimal and accurate system that integrates naturally with the bike without unnecessary bulk.

Implementation

Speed calculations were derived using wheel circumference measurements and kinematics principles from Grade 11 Physics.

Future Work

Future improvements include a 3D-printed enclosure and increased mechanical robustness.
